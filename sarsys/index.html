SARSYS

Search Management System

Key Features

SMS

Callout your team and any connected teams as well as passing information via SMS to your team members.

The Log

Anything that happens,anything you want to add, it goes in the Log, if its not there it never happend.

Resource Managament

Whether the incident requires Team management or individua resources. Sarsys enables you to manage these resources booking them into and out of incidents as well as allocate them to tasks.

Locate Me

For your misper that want to be found you can send them a message and if they choose to respond it will locate there location via their smart phone.

Contacts

Add and manage either global contacts, like your local POLSA's, other services etc. Also you can add incident specific contacts that are only available for that incident like volunteers from other organisations and such like.

Areas and Routes

Mark up search areas, routes and points for your teams to search and then send these to the allocated team/resources devices.
